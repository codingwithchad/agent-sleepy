Marketing & Influence Strategy: The
180-Day Sleep Shred
1. Concept & Persona
● The Brand: "The Tired Engineer" (or similar).
● The Hook: "I'm a 46-year-old backend dev who is sick of being tired. I'm building an AI
agent to fix my sleep so I can survive my 50th birthday."
● The Tone: No-nonsense, data-driven, slightly cynical, and results-oriented. Avoid
"hype-man" energy; lean into "hacker" energy.
2. Platform Strategy
● Instagram/TikTok: Short-form video (15-30s). Focus on the "Agent Output" (e.g., a
Telegram message roasting your caffeine intake) vs. your real-world reaction.
● Threads: Post-daily biometric snapshots and code snippets for the tech-curious.
● YouTube Shorts: "Day X of Y" updates. Fast-paced edits showing the Garmin graphs
and the Python terminal running.
